About

Welcome to The Royal Palace, where elegance meets comfort in the heart of Dhaka. Designed with the grandeur of royalty in mind, our hotel offers a luxurious escape from the ordinary. From our stunning architecture to the personalized service we provide, every detail is crafted to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.


At The Royal Palace, we believe that true luxury is about more than just lavish surroundings. It's about creating a welcoming environment where every guest feels like royalty. Whether you are here for business, leisure, or a special celebration, we offer a range of accommodations to suit your needs. Each of our rooms and suites are equipped with world-class amenities, providing you with the perfect blend of comfort and style.


Our hotel features a variety of services designed to enhance your experience, including an on-site restaurant serving gourmet cuisine, a fully-equipped spa for ultimate relaxation, and state-of-the-art conference facilities for business travelers.


Located in a prime area of Dhaka, The Royal Palace gives you easy access to local attractions, cultural landmarks, and vibrant shopping districts, making it the perfect base to explore the best of the city
