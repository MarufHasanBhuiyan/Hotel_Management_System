About The Grand Palace

Where Royal Heritage Meets Modern Luxury

Established in 2015, The Grand Palace stands as an iconic symbol of elegance and exceptional hospitality in the heart of the city. For nearly four decades, we have been crafting unforgettable experiences for discerning travelers from around the globe.

Our majestic architecture, inspired by classical palace design, houses 250 exquisitely appointed rooms and suites. Each space beautifully blends traditional grandeur with contemporary comforts, creating a sanctuary of refined luxury.
